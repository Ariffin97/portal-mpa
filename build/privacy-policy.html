Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

Introduction

Portal Pro is a private mobile application exclusively for Malaysia Pickleball Association (MPA) representatives and authorized personnel. This app is developed and maintained by Fenix Digital. All user accounts are created by Fenix Digital administrators.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal user data. All account information is created and managed by Fenix Digital. The app only stores authentication tokens locally on your device for login purposes.

App Usage

The app is used to manage tournament applications for the Malaysia Pickleball Association. Users can view, approve, or reject tournament applications through this administrative interface.

Data Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage. No personal data is transmitted or stored beyond what is necessary for authentication.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Expo for push notifications. No user data is shared with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
